# CORET – Monetization Strategy V1

## Primary Direction
Tools (Roadmap + Planning)

Not Depth. Not ML. Not a random mix.

---

## Why Tools

### Depth cannot be locked
Locking Alignment, Density, Cohesion, or Basic Optimize
removes all value from the free user.
Free version must feel legitimate and intelligent.

### ML is wrong primary driver
- Expensive to build
- Difficult to explain
- Can feel black-box
- Does not drive immediate payment willingness
ML is V2+. Not monetization basis at launch.

### Tools is natural Pro value
People pay for:
- Structured plan
- Future control
- Systematic progression
- Roadmap over time

Not for "seeing a score."
They pay to know what to do next.

---

## Free vs Pro

### Free (V1)
- Full CohesionEngine
- Basic Optimize (1 primary candidate)
- SeasonalEngine
- StructuralEvolution (phase + narrative)
- Wardrobe management

Free version is complete and worthy.

### Pro (V1.5+)

#### Roadmap Mode
- Multi-step optimize
- 2–3 steps ahead in structure
- Prioritized action sequence

#### Drift Detection
- Early instability alerts
- Structural friction tracking

#### Snapshot Compare
- Month-to-month analysis
- Component trend visibility

#### Advanced Density Tiering
- High-quality outfit scoring
- Structural depth insight

---

## What We Do NOT Do
- Do not lock Cohesion behind paywall
- Do not lock Optimize fully
- Do not lock Seasonal
- Do not create artificial friction

Free gives value.
Pro gives control.

---

## Conclusion

CORET monetizes primarily on:
Strategic tools and future planning.

Not on base measurement.
Not on AI magic.

Free = Structural understanding
Pro = Structural control
