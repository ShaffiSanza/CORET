# CORET – Business & Vision Document

## Core Identity

CORET is a Wardrobe Operating System.

It is not a fashion app.
It is not a trend engine.
It is not a shopping platform.

CORET measures and optimizes wardrobe structure.

The product exists to give users structural control over their clothing identity.

---

## Long-Term Vision

CORET aims to become:
The structural intelligence layer for personal wardrobe systems.

Long term, CORET can evolve into:
- A premium B2C SaaS
- A wardrobe intelligence API
- A structural optimization engine for commerce platforms

But first and foremost:
CORET must become a profitable, sustainable SaaS.

---

## Strategic Positioning

CORET sits closer to:
- Notion (structure)
- YNAB (control)
- Obsidian (system-thinking)

It does NOT compete with:
- Pinterest
- Zara / H&M
- Instagram fashion culture

CORET is about structure, not consumption.

---

## Business Model

Primary model: B2C subscription SaaS.

Free tier:
- Full structural measurement
- Basic optimize
- Evolution tracking
- Seasonal engine

Pro tier:
- Roadmap Mode
- Drift detection
- Snapshot comparison
- Advanced density analysis

Future Pro+:
- Behavioral overlay
- Environmental intelligence
- Cost-aware planning

Revenue target for sustainability: €1–3M ARR.

---

## Execution Philosophy

V1 – Stable deterministic core
V1.5 – Deeper structural tools (monetized)
V2 – Intelligent overlay
V3 – Platform expansion

Core engine remains deterministic and explainable.
No black-box AI replaces the structural model.

---

## Product Principles

1. Deterministic first
2. Explainable always
3. Control over hype
4. Structure over trend
5. Calm over stimulation
6. Premium over mass

---

## Monetization Philosophy

We do not lock basic measurement behind paywalls.
We monetize control and planning.

Users pay for:
- Clarity of next step
- Multi-step structural roadmaps
- Long-term system oversight

Not for basic scoring.

---

## Long-Term Optional Paths

If traction is strong:
- White-label engine licensing
- Commerce integration (structural role matching)
- Enterprise partnerships
- API exposure

These are not required for success.

---

## Success Definition

CORET is successful when:
- 5,000–10,000 paying users sustain the company
- The product is stable and trusted
- The engine is respected as structurally intelligent
- Users feel in control of their wardrobe identity

Exit is optional.
Sustainability is mandatory.

---

## Cultural Commitment

We build slowly, carefully, intentionally.

We do not chase trends.
We do not pivot impulsively.
We protect the system.

CORET is a long-term build.
