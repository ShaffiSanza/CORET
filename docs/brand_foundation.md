# CORET – Brand Foundation

## Name
CORET

## Primary Tagline
Your Wardrobe Operating System.

## Secondary Line
Built Around Your Core.

---

## Positioning

CORET is a personal wardrobe operating system that measures, optimizes, and evolves your wardrobe structure over time.

It is not a fashion inspiration app.
It is not a shopping platform.
It is not a budgeting tool.

CORET is a structural system.

---

## Core Philosophy

- Control over validation
- Structure over trend
- Identity over status
- Optimization over impulse
- Measurement without judgment
- Creative deviation allowed

CORET measures. The user decides.

---

## Emotional Core

Internal feeling: Control  
External effect: Cohesive presence  

---

## Target Audience

Primary:
Professionals aged 25–40
Value structure, clarity, and intentional identity

Secondary:
Style-conscious younger users

---

## Tone

- Calm
- Architectural
- Precise
- Gender-neutral
- Non-dramatic
- Non-preachy

---

## Visual Direction

Primary background: Warm dark taupe  
Cards: Light stone  
Accent: Deep muted forest green  
Animations: Soft, 200–300ms, ease-in-out  

Logo: CORET (uppercase, spaced typography)

---

## Product Identity

CORET is a long-term system, not a short-term style phase.

Seasonal recalibration supported.
Structural evolution tracked.
Rule-based engine in V1.
